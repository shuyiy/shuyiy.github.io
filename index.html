About

I'm an Interactive Media Design student at Algonquin College. My passions in video editing, graphic design and compositing have huge impacts in my life and career. I particularly enjoy working with CG and live action videos. My recent interests are photography, illustration, visual effects, compositing and animation. I hope you enjoy my works! If you have any question feel free to leave your message in the contact section.

I'm knowledgeable in Autodesk Maya, Foundry Nuke and Mari, Adobe Premiere, After Effects, Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign, VRay, Coding language (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, Python, C++).
